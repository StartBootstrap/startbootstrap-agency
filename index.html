Bitcoin payments everywhere

Payment Platform

Bitcoin Payment Processing for Payment Service Providers

Instant Exchange

Merchants are excited about accepting bitcoin payments, but don't want to be exposed to the bitcoin / fiat (EUR/GBP/USD) exchange rate volatility. We take care of this by instantly exchanging incoming bitcoin payments to fiat.

White Label

We provide a transparent bitcoin payment processing layer. PSPs control all processes like payment requests, merchant onboarding and reporting via our API. We also offer white-label merchant dashboards and payment pages.

Stable & Secure

Our experienced team of bitcoin and security experts makes sure our platform is well protected against denial of service attacks, hacking attempts and system downtimes. Regular audits ensure that we don't miss a thing.

About

Our story so far

  • September 2014

    Foundation

    Meinhard and Henning get together and outline a bitcoin payment platform. Kilian joins a little later and Deutsche Telekom's incubator hub:raum likes us so much that they offer to host us in their "hard working space". Thanks, hub:raum!

  • November 2014

    Accelerate!

    Axel Springer Plug and Play selects us for their 5th batch and invests in us. Callum and Victor help us to build the first iteration of our payment platform. A mad three months are starting.

  • January 2015

    Closed Beta Launch

    The acceleration program ends with a crowded demo day and we go live by connecting a handful of online merchants to our payment platform. First payments are processed and we start negotiating with payment service providers.

  • March 2015

    UK Expansion

    We are selected by London based Fintech Circle angel network as their first bitcoin start-up. Level39 mentor Nicolas joins us as an advisor and starts representing us in London.
