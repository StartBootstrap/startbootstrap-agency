What is Curling? 

\n

Kosovo's newest winter sport

\n

\n Originating from Scotland in 1511, Curling is a winter sport played on ice by over 1,500,000 people in 40 countries all over the world. Players take turns sliding 42lbs granite rocks towards a bullseye-looking target (the \"House\"). By adding a rotation on release, the rock will curve or \"curl\" as it slides down the ice. The goal is to get more of your teams stones closer to the center (the \"Button\") than your opponents.\n
