How it works

The KitSplit rental process in 6 easy steps.

  • Get started

    Sign up with Facebook or create an account and set up your profile.

  • Set up payments

    Add your payments profile to rent out equipment you own and get paid through our secure payment system managed by Braintree (owned by PayPal).

  • List

    Create new listings providing details and photos describing your equipment. You determine the price and decide when and where a renter can get the equipment.

  • Earn

    Renters will contact you to rent your listing, watch those dollar bill$$ roll in.

  • Find

    Search the KitSplit database of equipment listings to find the items you need. Search by keyword, category, or location, and request the dates and pick up the gear.

  • Coordinate

    Meet the owner, sign the KitSplit provided contract, and...

  • Make
    Something
    Awesome!
