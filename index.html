About

Yahoo Flickr Creative Commons 100M dataset consists of Flickr photos licensed under Creative Commons, uploaded from 2004-2014. Each record contains the photo's metadata, such as the date, location, and camera model.

Visualization Goals

Enable both camera-makers and analysts to explore the state and evolution of photo-taking activities. Specifically, the following visualizations show where Flickr users take photos, and with what device, over 2004-2014.

San Francisco

The following series of hexbin maps focuses on cities around the world. Each bin contains a number of photos taken at that location. The bin's hue is determined by the majority camera brand, its saturation by the ratio of the current bin's number of photos to the biggest bin's. Regional patterns of geolocations emerge from these maps. In San Francisco, many photos are taken in downtown, while not very many are taken in residential areas. Canon and Nikon users dominate traditional landscape locations–Golden Gate Bridge, Marin Headlands, and Ocean Beach, while Apple users are very active in downtown areas. Temporal patterns are equally interesting. Filter the time periods using the buttons below, and observe the growth of smartphones over time.
