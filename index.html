Services

Our Travel Consultants are all fully trained in providing the following services:

Flight Reservations

Travewave offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made.

Accommodation

Travewave takes pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally.

Car Hire

TraveWave is an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. However, should the customer prefer any other car rental company, Trip Travel will oblige.
