What is No Shy Club

An English and Soft Skills Club

FPT University

We are one of the biggest club in FPT University with many activities insize and outsize University.

English

We create an English environment for every member to learn and develop their English.

Soft Skills

Many events to develop member's soft skills, beside that we can closer to each other.

History

A ancient club in FPT University.

  • January 2010

    Our Beginnings

    No Shy CLub was founder by Quyen Nguyen on January 13, 2010; to create an open environment in which members have many opportunities to improve themselves in language & other soft skills!

  • February 2010

    The First Gen

    The first gen of No Shy Club include 20 members - Who built the foundation stone of our success today!

  • September 2010

    The First Event

    Being one of the first clubs in FPT University, We have the first event on September 2010. A successful event - our perfect beginning!

  • January 2020

    10 Year Journey

    After 10 year, January 13, 2020, A journey with all club's member, Overcoming many challenge, We are still here like a family - Who always stay together!
