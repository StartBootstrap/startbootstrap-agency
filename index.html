How it works

The KitSplit rental process in 5 easy steps.

  • 2009-2011

    Get started & set up payments

    Sign up with Facebook or create an account and then set up your profile. When creating your profile, make sure to add links to your social media accounts and say a little about what you do. This will help build trust with prospective renters and give them confidence that you’re not a dog. Then set up payments! In order to rent equipment you own and get paid, you must set up your payments profile. The payments profile is found under Your Name→ Settings→Payments.

  • List & Find

    Creating new listings takes only a few minutes! Provide details about your equipment and make sure to add a photo. We recommend setting a price between 5% and 10% of the current value. When someone wants to rent ,they'll send you an email or make a request. Set up a time and place to meet for the equipment handoff.
