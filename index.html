3053mm

measuring and marking tools

How many Measuring tools can be produce from fifty-three centimeters?
Series of a measuring and marking tools including: Caliber, Compass, True Angle, Measures Scope and leveler.
The project began searching for material that reflects the restlessness in me.
After examining materials I found that a Springy steel reflects exactly this feeling and it served as the basis for the realization of the idea.
The a Iconic Tape measure was used as a platform for creating the objects.

  • Materials: Three dimensional printing and Springy steel.
