Perfect Fit

Autism Resources by families for families.

Perfect is near and dear to my heart! This site was created from shear desperation of a more personal experience for families and individuals searching for resources for their loved ones. This site aims to bring a community together that sometimes could appear to be isolating and intimidating. Upon completion families will have list of resources and a solid community aimed at easing the load.

  • Date: April 2010
  • Perfect Fit
  • Technologies: BootStrap, Google Firebase, Google Maps API, GeoCode API, MySQL, & Nodejs

Top Shelf Crusaders

Recipe Generator.

This application is designed specifically for the hungry, lazy, foodie. Have you ever experienced a growling stomach with limited food in your pantry, and had a case of the driver blues. This where Top Shelf comes in. Just list the items you have on hand and this application will generate a number of recipes for you! Enjoy!!

  • Date: January 2018
  • Top Shelf
  • Technologies: Bootstrap, Chartjs, Edamam API, and more
